Kindred of Infinity
Existing together
Eternally apart
Dispersed in the wailing wind
Separated at birth
Clay surrounds hearts even thicker than the depth of the earth
Endless bodies like the seas and the skies
Are sooner to converge than you and I collide
We exist together while eternally apart
How vast the distance between who we are
Bond severed, an impassable chasm
A mystery to man
Circling the universe in tandem just out of reach
Because we beat upon on a wall instead of the door there is no answer
Are we destined to be on our own
Miles of night lay between twin stars
Are we only truly seen from afar
Kindred of Infinity we are eternally apart
